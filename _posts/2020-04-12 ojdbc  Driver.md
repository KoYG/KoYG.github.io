Oracle JDBC 드라이버
===
**JDK6*, *JDK7* 및 *JDK8*과 호환*
>
```
<dependency>
  <groupId>com.oracle.database.jdbc</groupId>
  <artifactId>ojdbc6</artifactId>
  <version>11.2.0.4</version>
</dependency>

**JDK8*, *JDK9* 및 *JDK11*과 호환*

>
<dependency>
  <groupId>com.oracle.database.jdbc</groupId>
  <artifactId>ojdbc8</artifactId>
  <version>19.3.0.0</version>
</dependency>
